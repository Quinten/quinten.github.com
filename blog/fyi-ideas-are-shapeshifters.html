FYI: ideas are shapeshifters

27-02-2024

When I leave an idea in my drawer for a while and I pick it up again, the idea has changed.

Ideas change. They are fluid. Ideas spawn other ideas. They travel across minds and keep transforming along the way.

The first humans who discovered ideas were valuable, feverishly started a landrush on them. But an idea is something very different than a square acre.

Ideas are shapeshifters.
